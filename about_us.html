According to individual projects and wishes the company develops and manufactures furniture for individuals, designers and commercial organizations.

We make luxury furniture

We follow the trends in the world of furniture, therefore we extend our range of natural stone slabs, sawn wood of noble species, stone decorations, as well as brass and stainless-steel furniture.

Advantages of our workshop

  • Own production of a complete cycle.
  • Manual treatment of each cut.
  • Achievement of any wood color. Casting colors are limited only by your imagination.
  • Turnkey product with a variety of materials.
  • We use French certified polymer in accordance with global eco-safety standards.
  • Excellent resin transparency, UV resistant.
  • Special casting technology that has been tested for years. Withstands high loads.
  • Hand polishing and buffing with special paste.
  • Ecological compatibility and safety of the polymer resin used in furniture manufacturing, confirmed by the report of Mosecomonitoring.

About us:

Hi! We love wood and all that can be made out of it. Where did our love begin? After working with many styles and trends, we've identified things that really inspire us.

We love real wood furniture and the way it blends seamlessly into the interior, the warmth and comfort it brings. We are fed up with "plastic" furniture, and we decided that we needed to create something timeless, so that once and for all.

Therefore we have launched a separate business - manufacture of designer furniture and interior items from wooden slabs.

We implement the ideas of our clients and our own to create the author's handmade furniture.

Why us? Because we tell our wooden stories to the world and we want our customers to feel happier with our furniture.

Slab furniture will look especially harmoniously in Scandinavian, Eco, Loft style interiors.
